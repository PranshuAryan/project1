The ArogyaSetu Idea

Self-Service Booking Screens Inside the Hospital Campus

Think of it like an ATM — but for doctor appointments. ArogyaSetu touchscreen booking screens are installed at multiple points inside the hospital campus. Patients walk in, find the nearest booking screen, and book their own appointment themselves — no queue, no counter, no waiting.

🖥️
Where are booking screens placed?

Multiple touchpoints across the hospital — so no single crowd forms

Hospital entrance lobby · OPD waiting area · Each department corridor · Emergency wing · Pharmacy area

🏥 Hospital Onboarding — One Time
1

Hospital Registers

Hospital admin signs up on ArogyaSetu, adds departments, timings and hospital layout.

2

Doctors & Slots Added

All doctors are listed with their specialisation, OPD days, timings and consultation fees.

3

Booking Screens Installed

ArogyaSetu booking screens (touchscreen + printer + payment) are deployed at key points across the campus.

4

System Goes Live

All booking screens sync in real time — slot availability, doctor schedules and payments are all connected.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Patient Self-Booking at Booking Screen
1

Patient Walks Up

Patient enters the hospital, spots the nearest ArogyaSetu booking screen and taps the screen to begin.

2

Enter Details & Cause

Patient fills in their name, age, phone number and selects their health concern or disease category.

3

Choose Doctor & Slot

Live doctor slots shown with AI-powered suggestions. Offline assistance available for the right choice.

4

Pay & Collect Appointment Receipt

Patient pays via UPI, card or cash. Appointment receipt is printed instantly with token number, doctor name and room.

💡

Why this is different from existing solutions

Online booking apps require internet, smartphones and digital literacy — which millions of Indians lack. ArogyaSetu booking screens are physically present inside the hospital, accessible to everyone — elderly, rural, first-time visitors — while still eliminating the queue at the counter.
